Should I be insulted?

Did someone say a mean thing to you on the Internet? Should you be offended, hurt and sad?
Paste the comment here and find out!

This is a machine learning model trained on data from the Jigsaw Toxic Comment Classification competition on Kaggle provided under CC0. The object is to distinguish comments or tweets with unwelcome content from the nicer ones, all by the moral standards provided by the training data. For entertainment purposes only!
Source code: github.com/peterahlstrom/NLP_toxic_classifier
